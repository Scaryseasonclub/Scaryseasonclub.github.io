Introduction

Ever since covid hit the world , We have lived with this fear inside of us knowing that anytime , we might die or someone we love might die . As a kid i was made to believe that ghosts and people with masks are the scariest and dangerous people in the world but growing up i have figured out that losing someone is even more scarier .

Why now

This covid pandemic has affected how we live and how we see the world , i for sure have started to appreciate my life and the good things in it . I recently watched a video about the poor oprhans in Africa that are struggling with food and living in the streets . As the pandemic started there has been a huge decrease in the number of donations to support these people hence I made this my own goal to help these less unfortunate kids to have better lives and something to live for .

In Africa there are estimated to be about 32 million orphans most of them have no oprhanage homes , they live in the streets. i made this my goal to raise money and give these little kids a better life , like how you and me are living , but i can't do it on my own . As a social worker student , yeah am still in university , it pains me to see all these kids in the streets dying of hunger .

We can all make a diffrence by partcipating in this project .
